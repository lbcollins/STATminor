The Statistics Minor Degree Plan

Five required courses (222, 224, 226 can be taken in any order; 227 requires 224)

  • STAT 224
  • One of STAT 222, 226, or 227
  • An approved elective (which can also be one of STAT 222, 226, or 2271)
  • A second approved elective
  • A third approved elective

One elective can even be an introductory course2 (STAT 2203 or 2344)5.

Caution! Note these important exceptions:

1 Do not take both STAT 226 and 227! Students may count either STAT 226 or 227, but not both, toward the Statistics Minor.

2 An introductory course may be used as an elective if not used to fulfill a requirement for any major(s), other minors, or general education requirements and if taken prior to any course for which at least STAT 220 or STAT 234 is prerequisite.

3 AP Statistics credit for STAT 220 cannot be used to fulfill a requirement for the Statistics minor.

4 AP Statistics caution: If you complete STAT 234, you forego your AP Statistics credit for STAT 220.

5 Do not take both STAT 220 and 234! Students may count either STAT 220 or 234, but not both, toward the total credits required for graduation from the College. If you do take both, then one course won’t count for anything at all …not even a general elective.
